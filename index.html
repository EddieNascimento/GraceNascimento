Grace Nascimento

" A girl with one goal, one dream! Play for England"

Take a look at Grace journey through her football career so far:

  • 2013 - Grace joined her hometown club Tenbury United U7s, on that same year she won "football player of the year" at the end of the season.
  • 2014 - Grace gone up to Tenbury United U8s, having another great season her passion for football grown even more, at the end of the season she won "parents Player of The Year".
  • 2015 - She moved up again now to U9s, at this time since joined Tenbury United she was the only girl in the team and also in the league, playing at the same level as the boys.
  • 2016 - Grace moved up again now to U11s, again Grace was shining through the season playing some amazing football, through the middle of the season, Grace received an invite to play for Leominster Town Girls U11s, she joined Leominster Town, playing for them on sundays and playing for Tenbury United on saturdays.
  • 2017 - Grace has been invited to be part of West Bromwich Albion Ladies RTC trials, 30 mins into the trial WBA Technical Director told Grace parents that the club want to give Grace a contract and sign Grace for the 17/18 U12s season. Grace again played some phenomenal football, she end up breaking the RTC goal record in one season scoring a total of 43 goals. Winning top scorer of the season!
  • 2018 - Grace then move up to play her first Year of U14s, another great season, Grace started to have an insight into style of play and more technical football. In the middle of the season Grace has been spotted by a England scout, so FA contact WBA and informed of Grace selection for the 3 days National Development Camp for U14s. Grace Scored another 27 goals through the season, finishing the season as a top scorer again.
  • 2019 - Now Grace moved to her first year of U16s, again start the season well, been selected for England Camp again Grace started developing new positions playing as a right and left wing. Then covid 19 hit England, so the whole country went on full lockdown. When the season re-started Grace been offered an opportunity to Sign for Aston Villa FC, which is one of the best RTCs in the country. So in the middle of the 19/20 season Grace sign a contract with Aston Villa Women's.
  • 2020 - Grace Finishd playing the 20/21 season with villa, getting used to her new club and playing great football.
  • 2021 - After a difficult start of the season due to covid the 21/22 season was underway, Grace started the season really well, again developing new posittions, this time as a left and right back. then after one month into the season Grace received another call ups for England Camp U16s one in August, one in October and the other one in December.
  • 2022 - Grace started the year well, receiving another call up for England Camp this time being selected to be officially part of Women's U16 squad at St George's Park. then afterwards been selected to be part of England WU16s Development Tournament in Portugal, in which she had the opportunity to fulfil her dream in palying for England, playing an official match against portugal and Germany.
